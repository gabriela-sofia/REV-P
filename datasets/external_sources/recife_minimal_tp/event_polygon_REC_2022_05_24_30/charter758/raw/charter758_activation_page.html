Landslides in Brazil

In Brazil, civil defense officials reported torrential rains left many people dead or missing.

The disaster is the latest in a recent series of deadly landslides and floods to hit the country triggered by extreme weather.

Authorities warned the forecast of more rain, but while the storm subsided the search and rescue work resumed.
